About Virgin America

California-based virgin America(VX) is a low-cost carrier that flies to more than 20 destinations within the United states and Mexico.The airline's fleet is configured with two classes of services:first class and Main cabin(Economy Class).Within the main cabin,passengers can opt for Main cabin(Economy class).Within the Main Cabin,Passengers can opt for Main Cabin Select Seats.Thease seats which are located at the bulkheads and exist rows,offer more legroom.Virgin America operates hubs at San Francisco International Airport(SFO) and Los Angeles International Airport(LAX).First Class and Main Cabin Select Passengers can purchase passes to the airline's three Virgin America Clubhouse lounges or the Virgin America Loft at LAX.
