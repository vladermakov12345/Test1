To make adjustments to your reservation after booking, please call Priceline at 1-(877) 477-7441.

You must call the hotel directly to request an Accessible Room. As part of our Vision for accessible travel, we hope to offer guaranteed accessible rooms in the future. You help make this possible by booking through accessibleGO!

When you book at least 48 hours prior to your stay, we send a personal email to the hotel management with a list of your accessibility needs and special requests.

This is a free service for booking with accessibleGO. However, we cannot guarantee that the hotel will fulfill your requests.
